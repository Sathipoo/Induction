These black or white sesame seeds, rich in oil and aroma, are then cleaned gently to remove dust or debris.

🛢 Wood-Pressed the Traditional Way (Chekku Nune)

  • ● A small amount of palm jaggery or dry ginger is sometimes added — to balance heat and enhance shelf life (as per tradition)
  • ● The oil slowly begins to flow — dark golden, thick, and aromatic

This process preserves the natural flavor, nutrients, and medicinal qualities of the sesame oil — something no refined oil can offer.
